Here lies Toloje

The year 1066, when a boar was master in a forest, and I ...

Sulduk

A se leži Sulduk a usiče kam otac.
Bože, mir daj snu duše njegove, a Ti i ja poravnajmo ratčune. Vas ste troje u jednome, a ja sam sada sam, ali i sam ne bojim se vas troice.
Ako je nepravda Božija volja, tada je moja sudba biti protiv Boga. Človjekov sine ne privali ovi kam sina mojega on je spomen, i nek takav i ostane, Božije greške. Za mir moje duše ne pitaj ona svoje smirenje više ne može nać ni na ovom ni na onom svjetu. 1135. ljeto

Here lies Sulduk, and his father cut the stone.
God, give peace to dreams of his soul, and You and I shall settle the score. There are three of you in one, and I am alone now, but even alone I am not afraid of three of you.
If injustice is God's will, then my destiny must be against God. Son of a man, do not tumble this stone of my son, it is a memory of, and let it stay as it is, God's mistake. For peace of my soul do not ask, it cannot find its peace in this or other world.
The year 1135.

Knez Nenc

Se znamenje Kneza Nenca,Velikoga Kneza Bosanskoga, a postavi je sin njegov Knez Muven, s`Božijom pomoćju i svojih vjernih, a s` inonom nijednom inom pomoćju, nego on sam.
Ti, koji pročitaš moj kam, možda si hodio do zvijezda. i vratio se, jer tami neima ništa, do ponovo ti sam. Človjek mojže vidjeti ono tšto nije vidio, tčuti ono što nije tčuo, okusti ono što nije otkusio, bit tami gdji nije bio, al` uvijek i svagdi samo sebe može najti, ili ne najti. I mnogo ot moje ruke na zemji bi, a ni ot mene niko ne bi mrtav i ubit. I da ostavih kosti u tujini, i tad bih samo Bosnu sanjo. Človječe, tako da niesi proklet, ne tikaj u me.
Legoh 1094 ljeta, kad bješe suša, pa u nebu ne bješe ni jedne suze za me.


This is a stone of Duke Nenc, Great Duke of Bosnia, placed by his son Duke Muven, with the help of God and those loyal to him, and with no other help, but he himself.
You that read my stone might have walked to the stars. And you came back, for there is nothing there but you alone. A man can see what he hasn't seen, hear what he hasn't heard, taste what he has not tasted, and be there where he has not been, but always and anywhere, only can find or not find himself. And a lot there was by my hand, but not one dead or killed. And if I left my bones in a foreign country, still I would dream only about Bosnia. Oh man, do not be cursed, do not touch me.
I lay down in the year 1094 when it was a drought, so there was not a single tear in the sky for me.

Didodrag Gojak

A se leži Didodrag Gojak, na svojini, na plemenitoj u Zahumlju.
Zašto me mati rodi?
Jer, ovdi ima višlje lažnog sunčevog zlata no snova, višlje vjetra no dobrih riječi, višlje praznine no ljubavi, višlje laži no istine, višlje uzimanja no davanja. Jer, dani ovdi nisu ispunjeni vrimenom vetć morom, notći nisu zdjele pune slatkih sanja vetć burad puna gortčine.
Zašto me mati ti rodi?
Da budnem žedan, da budnem gladan, umoran, tujžan,....i da me ti vode napojiš, sirom i hljebom nahraniš, čistom posteljom odmoriš, osmijehom razveseliš. O mati, sve bi to bilo isto, a potpuno drugatčije, da to hotće da uradi Kosara. Al` ona to netće. Hotće, al` drugomu. A ja hotću samo nju.
I zbog toga zgiboh. Al`, mati, nije to bilo zalud. Jer, kad ona budne i stara i ružna i zla, i kad se za njom, mati, nitko ne budne okretao, a onaj njen Juroje je iz kutće istjera, i kad ne budne imala ni gdje ni kud, tad će se mene mlada sjetiti, mene i moje ljubavi. Al` ja tad, mati, iz neba nejću doć. Ozgo tću sve to samo glidati. Zbog tog zmreh majko mlad. Al` zbog toga budutćeg trena, vridlo me i rodit. Ti putniče, koji moju patnju sada znaš, ne privali mi biljega, a privališ li ga Bog ti jos terži na tvoje tijelo stavio.
1167 godne, kad bjehu pune vlkova šume.


Here lies Didodrag Gojak, on his land, a noble one in Zahumlje.
Why did you, mother give birth to me?
Because there is more false sunshine gold here than dreams, more wind than pleasant words, more emptiness then love, more lies than truth, more taking than giving. Because days here are not filled with time but with nightmares and nights are not bowls filled with sweet dreams but barrels full of bitterness.
Why did you, mother give birth to me?
To be thirsty, to be hungry, tired, sad….and that you give me water, to feed me with cheese and bread, to rest me in a clean bed, and to cheer me with a smile. Oh mother, all that would be the same, but completely different, if Kosara wanted to do that. But she doesn't. She wants but for someone else. And I, I want only her.
And for that, I died. But, mother, that was not in vain. For when she gets old and ugly and evil, and when nobody, mother turns to look at her anymore, and when her Juroje ousts her from the house, and when she has nowhere to go, then she will remember me young, me and my love. But then mother, I will not come from the skies. I will just look at that from above. For that mother, I died young. But for that future moment, it was worthed to be born. You traveler, who know my sufferings now, do not tumble my stone, and if you do, may God put an even heavier one onto your body.
The year 1167, when forests were full of wolves.

Trtiša

A sej je kam od Trtiše.
Živjeh mirno, Boga moleć i zla ne misleć. Ovden, gdi mi je kam, ubi me grom. Zašto Bože? Da su klete i proklete ruke koje bi ovo preturile dok odgovor ne dobijem.
1174 godne po Gospodu našem.


This is a stone of Trtisa.
I lived in peace, praying to God and thinking of no evil. Here, where my stone is, lightning stroke me Why, God? Cursed and damned those hands that tumble this until I get the answer.
The year 1174 of Our Lord.

Knez Rastudije

A sej leži počteni knez Rastudije Prvonjeg na svojoj zemji na plemenitoj.
U to doba bjeh junakom, mil bratiji i gospodinu Kulinu. Najbolji muž u Dubravah bih. Ondak kad sam mogao nisam htio, sada kada hotću ne mogu ratširiti krila. Takva mi kob. Nisam otčajavao onda netću ni sada.
Al vidim da svi koji lete slete i da niko ne osta zauvijek u nebu sa svojom hrabrostću pa netću ni ja dovijeka ispod ovog kamena sa svojijem strahom. Pristupite i žalite me, al ne popirajte me nogami, jer tćete biti vi kako jesam ja, a ja netću biti kakovi jeste vi. Usijetče Veseoko Kukulamović a pisa Gost
1203. oko Velike Gospojine.


Here lies honest duke Rastudije First of his name, on his land, noble one.
At that time, I was a hero, dear to my brothers and lord Kulin. Best husband in Dubrave I was. Then, when I could have I did not want, and now when I want I can not spread my wings. That is my fate. I was not in despair then, and I won't be now.
But I see that all that fly, land, and no one stayed in skies forever with his courage, so shall not I stay forever under this stone with my fear. Come and mourn me, but do not step over me, for you will be as I am now, and I will not be as you are. Cut by Veseoko Kukulamovic, and inscribed by Gost
The year 1203, around Assumption Day.

Bogčin Radinić

A se ne leži junak Bogčin Radinić iz Bosne Srebrne.
Tugdje na njegovoj plemenitoj baštini uz kameni biljeg otca mu i djeda i djedovog djede djeda, leži samo biljeg njegov, jer je on zagibo i zalegho u tudjoj zemlji, tamor gdji je i Sonce drugatčije i vjeter drugatčiji i Bog i voda i vazduh i ljudi tudji i duši mojoj strani. Kulinu, matčhu i kopju bjeh vjerniji no Radaci i ni mi togda stid ni žal. I kadah bih bil, kakor vi jeste, opeta bih bil isti kakor ja bih. Vi nikdar nećte biti kakor ja, a ja ne mogu biti ko tšto nekdar bi. Blagosloven ko pročita i zmisli se, a lud koji privali.
Ljeta 1205, godnu nakon tšto v zemju leže Veliki Ban Kulin...


Here lies a hero Bogcin Radinic from Bosnia Srebrna.
There at his noble land, by the stone of his father and his grandfather, and his grandfather's grandfather, lies only his stone, for he fell and lied down in a foreign land, there where the sun is different and the wind different, and the God and water and air and people foreign and strange to my soul. To Kulin, a sword and a spear I was faithful more than Radaci and I'm not ashamed or sad for that And if I was like you are, again I would have been the same as I was. You will never be like me, and I can't be what I used to be. Blessed who reads and wonders, and mad who tumbles.
The year 1205, a year after Great Ban Kulin lay into the ground.

Stanac

A se leži Stanac Godinov sin, na svojini, na plemenitoj, v Bosni.
Ne ubi me sedam rani od strijela, kopja i sjekre, ne proždra me ni jena šumska zvijer, ne proguta me riječni vir, ni oganj ni zima mi ne zmetoše trag. Ubi me Sipara jerbo mi se obećala a za drugog otšla. Prosto joj bilo sve, al ja tću našeg sina i dalje da sanjam, makar sada sam. Ne zvalite mi ovi kami. To su moje otči tkroz koje i daljše zvijezde i Siparu gledam.
1209. ljeto v listopad mjesec lijep, al tujžan, jer nijma višlje onog tšo bi, a nijma ni onog tšo tće nekad opet bit.


Here lies Stanac, son of Godin, on his land, a noble one, in Bosnia.
Seven wounds of arrows, spear, and sword did not kill me, no beast of forest ate me, no whirlpool of river swallowed me, no fire or winter ruined my tracks. Sipara killed me, for she promised herself to me, and went with another. I forgive her all, but I will dream about our son, even all alone now. Do not tumble these stones. These are my eyes through which I still look at the stars and Sipara.
The year 1209, a month of October is beautiful, but sad, for there is no more the one that was, and there is no one that will be again.

Ljubljen

A se leži Ljubljen, u Vrhbosni rozdjen, u Vrhbosni zakopan, na svojini na plemenitoj.
Ja bjeh onaj tkoji cijel život na raskrsnicam stajah, razmišljah, oklijevah. Bjeh onaj tkoji se pitah: kak to da nebo ne stari, a iz njeg se stalno razdaju nova i nova godišnja doba. I u sobi, gdje bjeh, bješe prozor, a iza prozora beskraj. Al' ja uporno gledah u pod. I mišljah moju smerti sve treba to konačno stati. Al' nije, i moja smert sve starša i sve tješnja je. Kam mi usječe Dražeta, a zapis upisa Husan, ne da pokažu da bjeh, vetć da me višlje neima.
Ljeta Gospodnjeg 1258.


Here lies Ljubljen, born in Vrhbosna, buried in Vrhbosna, on his land noble one.
I was the one that stood at crossings all my life wandering and hesitating. I was the one that asked himself: how come the sky does not grow old, and new seasons are always coming from it? And in a room, where I was, there was a window, and behind the window was infinity. But I persistently looked at the floor. I thought that with my death all that needs to stop finally. But it didn't, and my death is getting older and tighter. The stone is cut by Drazeta, and inscribed by Husan, not to show that I was, but that I am no more there.
Year 1258 of Our Lord.

Mišljen

A se leži Dobri Gost Mišljen, prave vjere Bosanske.
Biljeg postavi kšći Korija i na mi i na mojim milu kućnicu Badaču. Ti, koji prolaziš, projdi u miru i ne spominji si i ne gonetaj grijehe naše. Zalud ti je taj posao. Naši su dani izbrojani, naše notći potrošene, naši grijesi dim. Svojih koraka se plaši, oni tće ti glave dojći, na putu kojim hodiš. I da znaš, više vrijedi crv koji se sad kretje, no sva dobra djela koja zajedno učinismo za života svojih i Badača i ja.
Kam nam usjecen godne 1273 po Gospodu u Kočerini, na plemenitoj zemji, a pisa Dabiša.


Here lies Good Gost Mišljen, of right fate Bosnian.
The stone is placed by his daughter Korija, to me and my dear wife Badaca. You, that pass by, pass in peace and don't mention or try to unriddle our sins. It is vain work. Our days are counted, our nights are spent, and our sins are smoke. Be afraid of your steps, for they will take your life, on the path you are walking. And know this, a worm that moves now is worth more, than all deeds that Badaca and I made in our lives.
A stone is cut in the year 1273 of Our Lord, in Kocerina, in noble land, and inscribed by Dabisa.

Prijezda Viganj

A se leži Prijezda Viganj, na svojini, na plemenitoj ukraj Jajca.
Hodih, al` ne dojdoh. Gradih, al` ne zgradih. Sadih, al` ne žnjeh. Govorih, al` ne iskazah. Voljeh, al` ne bijah voljen. Sad odoh, a ne piše mi se za vratit. A stio bih, jer ništ ne završih. Ne ticaj mi ovi kam, jerbo tće isti Bog na tvoje kostji stavit. Ni ti nisi završil sve što si zmislil.
1303 po vremenu Gospodina, a Stjepana Bana trinaesta v Bosni..


Here lies Prijezda Viganj, on his land, a noble one near Jajce.
I walked but arrived not. I built but finished not. I sowed but reaped not. I spoke but said not. I loved but was loved not. Now I leave, and it does not say I will come back. But I would like to, for I finished nothing. Do not touch this stone of mine, for God will put the same onto your bones. You have not finished all you thought either.
The year 1303, of Our Lord, and Stjepan Ban XIII in Bosnia.

Dinko Vlkač

A se leži Dinko Vlkač, mladji od dvaest proletja, a berži od jesenjeg vjetra.
Ubi me na nevjeru Semrad, lojš sin od gorjega otca Zleba izdanek. Božje, Ti ak hoš, i ak mož, oprosti im. Ja nit molgu, nit hotju, nit njem, ni teb ni ojnom kog kam ovi pokriva. Kam usijetče Vitača, a biljeg upisa Miltoš vojenom ruhkom, ali samo ono tšto mu ona rijetče. Bože, ako ovud projdež i projtčitaš Dinkov kam još dulgo ćemo se glijdati u otči.
U godini Gospoda našega 1327, kad u Bosni bješe Stjepan Kotromanić Ban..


Here lies Dinko Vlkac, younger than twenty springs, and faster than autumn wind.
I was killed tricky by Semrad, the bad son of a worse father Zleb, offspring. God, if You want and can forgive them. I neither can, nor want, nor him, nor you nor the one that this stone covers. The stone is cut by Vitaca, and Miltos inscribed it with his loving hand, but just what she told him. God, if you pass this way and read Dink's stone, we will look each other in the eye for a long time.
Year 1327 of Our Lord, when Stjepan Kotromanic was Ban in Bosnia.

Taniša Crk

Here lies Tanisa Crk, from Kraljeva Sutjeska, loved by the King, but with no freedom, as a hound dog given to the King.
I lived, but did not slaked thirst with water, nor allayed hunger with fruits of the land, because hunger and thirst returned every day to my stomach, as I returned to my home from the field, the same but different for that day. And I thought of You, God, and with prayer to You, I closed my eyes in the night, and with prayer to You I opened them in the morning, like the windows and doors of your home and mine, open in the morning. And I waited for you all the time, and hoped. But You did not come nor appeared. Just the silence. And a doubt was born in my soul, not inclined to doubt, that You somewhere, like me here, are waiting in vain salvation from me. And with that heavy thought I lied under this stone and inscribed that thought in hard stone, so those that read it can see which one of us will receive salvation first.
I lied bitter in year 1389 of Our Lord, when Tvrtko was king of Bosnia, Serbia, Dalmatia and Western Sides, and I was an old man which have seen in the world what I did not want to see, and did not see what my heart always waited and just that wanted.

Linil

A se leži Linil, Gostja Hotjena najstarši sin.
Mlogo sam hotjeo, al sad vidjim da liscem u lisce s` kami nejma razlika izmedju lisca. I dervo mojže bit ogromno ko hum, al` listje mu pajda uz korijenje. Kuda ijdeš človječe? Zar ti je vetja pamet u noghami no u glavji?!
Biljeg usjetče najstaršem bratju najmladji Borjen, a pisa dijak Stanko. V Vrhbosni ljeta Gospodina našeg 1402.. .


Here lies Linil, of Gostja Hotjena eldest son.
I wanted much, but now I see that face to face with a stone, there is no difference between faces. A tree can be big as a hill, but its leaves fall to its roots. Where are you going, human? Is your wisdom greater in legs than in your head?! The stone is cut to the eldest brother, by the youngest brother Borjen, and inscribed by a learner Stanko.
In Vrhbosna, in the year 1402 of Our Lord.

Ljuben Dragota

A se leži Ljuben Dragota na svojini na plemenitoj.
Zmreh i ni mi žal. Terško mi je tšto ne prejskotčih zijd vrejmena, tšto nejmadoh tri sin i tri tšćerji pa da se ušestostrujčen vrejmenu izrugujem. Tog mi žal a ni življenja. Ne krejti u moj kam, jer ja nikgoh nejmam da ga obaljenog vrejti. Ispod nisu samo moje kostji vetć moja zla sudba koja nejkog da zjaši vrejba.
1405. po Gospodu godne .


Here lies Ljuben Dragota, on his land, a noble one.
I died and I do not regret it. I'm sad that I did not jump over the wall of time, that I have not had three sons and three daughters, so I can mock the time multiplied by a hundred. I am sorry about that, but not living. Do not tumble my stone, for I have nobody to lift it if down.
Year 1405 of Our Lord.

Asta

A se leži Asta, Bogčina Zloušića kšći, a ne leži mi se.
Kako bih rado sad pred vetče s tebom krojz livade projšetala i onaj poljubac što si isko, a ne dobio, ti dala. Pa da i nebo pukne. Ne bi mi bilo žal, ni stid. Namjerniče, ne tiči mi kam, nek oni koji netčine utčine tšto ja ne utinih, jer ja tek sad znam kako dušu perže neispunjena davanja.
1422 ljeta, kad se radovaše sretni, a ja zmreh.


Here lies Asta, daughter of Bogscin Zlousic, but I do not want to lie.
Oh how I would like to walk the meadows with you in the sunset and give you that kiss that you asked for, but did not get. Even if the sky bursts. I would not regret or be ashamed. Stranger, do not touch my stone, let those that do not do, do what I did not, for now I know how the soul is burned by unfulfilled giving.
The year 1422, when happy ones rejoiced, and I died.

Krkša i Kalija

A se leže Krkša i Kalija na svojini, na plemenitoj u Ključu.
Voljesmo se i iz ljubavi postasmo muž i žena i Bog nam dade mnogo djece. A onda dvaest godin poslje našeg prvog zajedničkog listopada gledasmo dugu i vidjesmo da je vidimo različito i poželjesmo različite želje. I zatčudismo se da nas zajednički život utčini različitijim nego što ranije bjesmo. Kad Krkša zmre, za njim isti dan zmre moje srdce i zmroh i ja. Ne preturi nam ovi kami, jer nam se i sad na mjesetčini kostji raspravljaju ko je i kolko u pravu, a ko ne, pa i u smerti svojoj postajemo još i vetći stranci, no što u življenju bjesmo. Al' ako nam kostji razdvojiš jedno bez drugog bi zmreli ponovo i konatčno i u našoj drugoj smerti.
1447 godine, kad Kraljem Bosne bješe Stjepan Toma(š) Ostojić.


Here lie Krksa and Kalija, on their land, a noble one in Kljuc.
We loved each other, and for that we became husband and wife and God gave us many children. And then, twenty years after our first autumn together, we looked at the rainbow and saw that we see it differently and we wished different wishes. And we were surprised that our life together had made us more different than we were before. When Krksa died, my heart died after him the same day, so I died too. Do not tumble this stone, because even now our bones argue in the moonlight on who is right and how much and who is not, and even in our death we become even bigger strangers than we were alive. But if you separate our bones, without each other we would die again and forever in our second death.
In the year 1447, when Stjepan Toma(s) Ostojic was King of Bosnia.
